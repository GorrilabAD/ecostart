СУТЬ ПРОЕКТА

Мы создаем EcoStart - это публичная распределённая криптографическая платформа,

которая послужит для создания приложений и программных решений на открытом блокчейне по финансированию экологических и природоохранных проектов, с собственной криптовалютой TECO, при генерации которой используется алгоритм Proof-of-Importance.

Проект Ecostart направлен на решение одной из острых проблем всего человечества – экологической, используя современные информационные технологии для создания и функционирования системы финансирования и реализации экологических мероприятий.

TECO BLOCKCHAIN

Инвестиции в жизнь

Мы создаем экосистему основанную на собственной Blockchain технологии, которая обеспечит полную прозрачность сбора и контроля расходовывания средств для экологических проектов, отсутствие посредников, зависимость от органов государственной власти и глобальных финансовых групп.

Благодаря TECO будут решаться острые экологические проблемы по всему миру, путем финансирования стартапов держателями токенов, а также с помощью собственной технологии Blockchain, где комиссия с каждой транзакции отправляется в фонд по защите огружающей среды.

TECO COMMUNITY

Экологическое сообщество

Встроенный в экосистему криптографический мессенджер позволит обмениваться сообщениями между всеми участниками, обсуждать экологические проблемы и формировать идеи по их устронению.

Любой участник сообщества может принимать участие в сервисе по экспертизе проектов, где выявляются неэкологические проекты, формируется список рекомендаций, рейтинг доверия и репутации.

TECO APPS

Экологичские приложения

На базе TECO Blockchain любой участник экосистемы может создавать собственные приложения для решения экологических проблем.

Логику работы приложений будут обеспечивать смарт контракты, а интеграцию со сторонними ресурсами открытое API для разработчиков.

What is the EcoStart Ecosystem?

Ecostart is an innovative solution for the global market for environmental projects. Ecostart is implemented in the form of a public distributed cryptographic platform for creating applications and software solutions on an open block system to support environmental and nature protection projects. Issue of EcoCoin crypto currency will be the first step towards our goal. It will be followed decentralized investment environmental fund (тикер IEF), fundraising application - service for creating a personal digital token and more.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to completely democratize process of investment of environmental projects by eliminating intermediaries and placing the power and control where it belongs - entirely into the hands of investors and ECN crypto currency holders.

How is a IEF Fund different from a traditional investment fund?

A IEF Fund invests in crypto assets only and does not have a central power of authority. There is no fund manager to make decision on behalf of investors. This is where you can feel the power of the wisdom of the crowd. All participants are given the chance to vote and to decide the future of the next investment.

When will the EcoStart platform be launched?

EcoStart Beta will be launched in Q4 2018 The official launch of the EcoStart Platform will follow in Q2 2019

Do you have a prototype?

Our platform unites a number of serious tech solutions in a single system. Some parts of the technology already exist and are being employed. As of now we have aerospace monitoring application of the earth's surface.

What currencies are accepted for payment?

The following currencies are accepted for payment: Ethereum (тикер ETH), Bitcoin (тикер BTC) Litecoin (тикер LTC) and US Dollar (тикер USD).

Which countries or nationalities are eligible to purchase ТЕТ tokens?

Citizens of any country except USA may take part in the project.

I transferred ETH to your a ccount, but while the transaction was being processed, ICO ended. What will happen to my ETH?

If the ETH is transferred to the project's wallet after the ICO is finished, it is returned to the sender's wallet according to the current exchange rate with no conversion. For example, after the ICO is completed, if the project wallet is funded with 1 ETH, the same amount will be returned to the sender's wallet, disregarding the ETH quote at the moment the ETH was sent. To prevent such situations, we recommend setting the appropriate transaction commission for timely processing and considering the ICO timeframe. The same process will be activated for any other cryptocurrency.

What is the minimum target? What happens if the required amount is not raised?

Minimum target equals $1 million. If the amount is not raised, we will return all the raised funds back to the wallets of the crowdsale participants. cryptocurrency.

When will the bounty payouts take place?

Bounty payouts will be paid to the participants within one week after the ICO.

When will your tokens be listed on exchanges?

We plan to list ТЕТ tokens on several major exchanges within one month after the ICO.

Which blockchain will you use?

At the current stage, we will use Ethereum smart contracts. Additionally, we are not platform or instrument dependent (we are not tied to a particular platform or instrument in the blockchain). We will create our own blockbuster platform that will match that best meets our requirements for speed, reliability, and cost.

What is the service of creating a personal digital token?

The service for creating personal digital tokens and ICO will provide an opportunity for environmental project initiators to create their own token using the easy-to-use interface of the EcoStart platform. The initial offer of coins will allow to participate in financing the project literally to all comers. The issue of own tokens will be carried out on the basis of the EchoCoin crypto currency protocol. ECT Tokens can serve as a product token, electronic cryptovexel, share in the project and participation certificates. For each token, you can specify your own name, output volume, number of decimal places and other parameters and properties.
